## What is Career Optional?

Career Optional is a podcast about one idea: the same skill set — agile thinking combined with AI automation — works whether you stay corporate or go indie.

This isn't about quitting your job. It's about having the option to.

I'm Danny Liu. In January 2023, I was laid off. Two weeks later, I had a new role at a Fortune 500 — not because of my certifications, but because I had exactly the Jira automation and business analytics skills they were hiring for.

That experience changed how I think about career security. The goal isn't to keep a job. The goal is to build skills that make employment one option among many.

**Five episodes a week. Monday through Thursday, AI-produced. Friday, my real voice.**

Welcome to Career Optional.
