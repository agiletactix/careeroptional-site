## The Layoff That Wasn't a Crisis

In January 2023, Danny got laid off from a Fortune 500 bank. The job family got cut. He went with it.

Two weeks later he had a new offer. Not because he got lucky — because for years he had been building skills that lived outside his job title.

This episode is the story of what actually happened — and why the layoff wasn't a crisis.
